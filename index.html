About Me

I go by the name 999Legend's, an upcoming rapper from South Africa. Music has been part of my life for as long as I can remember — I started rapping back in 2018, when I was just six years old.

I’ve always looked up to Juice WRLD, who inspired me to chase music even at a young age. When he passed away in 2019 at only 21, I almost gave up on my dream. But when songs like Godzilla by Eminem and albums like Legends Never Die and Fighting Demons came out, I realized his legacy was bigger than life itself. That’s when I decided to carry that same energy forward — and that’s where the name Legend comes from.

The journey hasn’t been easy. In 2023, I lost my mom’s phone that held more than 300 songs I recorded in studios. It felt like starting over after investing so much time and money. For a while, I gave up on music completely. But in 2024, when I got a new phone, I discovered BandLab, a mobile app that lets me record my own tracks. Since then, I’ve been writing, recording, and building my sound again.

Now, at 13 years old, I’m ready to share my music with the world. Some songs will stay unreleased, but many are coming out on platforms like SoundCloud. My goal is simple: to turn my story into sound, to connect with people who feel the same struggles, and to keep building on the legacy that inspired me in the first place.
